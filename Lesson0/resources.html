Online tutorials

So, you’ve decided to learn programming. You're excited and ready to learn, and then you ask yourself, “Where do I begin, and which language do I start with?” My main advice for anyone wanting to start is to do exactly that — just start. As soon as you get your hands dirty and actually write some code and get it running, you will already have gained an enormous amount of knowledge. Don’t be afraid to make mistakes — some of the most important things I’ve learned have come from breaking my own code.
